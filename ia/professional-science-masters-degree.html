Professional Science Master’s Degree in Information Assurance
Norfolk State University is in the process of developing a Professional Science master’s (PSM) degree in Information Assurance (IA). This will be a two year program designed to allow students to excel in cybersecurity, while at the same time developing the management, ethical and legal training needed in the information assurance workforce.


The PSM in IA at Norfolk State University will be developed in concert with industry partners who will provide guidance during the development of the PSM degree and will provide internships and permanent employment for graduates of the program.
