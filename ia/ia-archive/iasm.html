Norfolk State University changed its admission criteria to enhance student credentials for college and motivate high school students toward higher achievement. Effective, Fall 2001, the University implemented the following admission standards - GPA 2.3, SAT 800 and 17 ACT.

Academic progress toward a degree for a student is determined by the student's academic standing as measured by the number of semester hours graded, with grades of "A" through "F", and the number of grade points earned.  A minimum of 240 grade points and 120 graded semester hours (2.0 or "C" average) are required for graduation.
