\tab\tab\tab\tab <p><b><font size="4">\par
\tab\tab\tab\tab <img border="0" src="Kim%20Luckes%20photo.jpg" width="200" height="300" align="left" hspace="12" vspace="5" alt="Kim Luckes, J.D."></font></b></p>\par
\tab\tab\tab <br>\tab <p><b><font size="4">Kim Luckes, J.D.</font></b><br>\par
\tab\tab\tab\tab <br>\par
\tab\tab\tab\tab Kim Luckes (LEW-kus), J.D., the acting president of Norfolk \par
\tab\tab\tab\tab State University, previously served as the liaison to the NSU \par
\tab\tab\tab\tab Board of Visitors and assistant to the president. Luckes holds a \par
\tab\tab\tab\tab bachelor\rquote s degree from Elizabeth City State University and a \par
\tab\tab\tab\tab juris doctor degree from North Carolina Central University\rquote s \par
\tab\tab\tab\tab School of Law. Additionally, she is a graduate of Leadership \par
\tab\tab\tab\tab North Carolina, Class XII (2005) and has participated in the \par
\tab\tab\tab\tab Mabel Parker McLean Women\rquote s Leadership Development Forum of the \par
\tab\tab\tab\tab United Negro College Fund, Inc. (April 2004).<br>\par
\tab\tab\tab\tab A native of Newport News, Va., Luckes brings more than 20 years \par
\tab\tab\tab\tab of administrative experience in higher education to Norfolk \par
\tab\tab\tab\tab State. Prior to NSU, she served as executive vice president at \par
\tab\tab\tab\tab Saint Augustine\rquote s College in Raleigh, N.C. As Executive Vice \par
\tab\tab\tab\tab President, she was responsible for the areas of institutional \par
\tab\tab\tab\tab effectiveness, personnel management, and strategic planning and \par
\tab\tab\tab\tab assessment. Additionally, Luckes was responsible for the \par
\tab\tab\tab\tab day-to-day operations at Saint Augustine\rquote s College. Before \par
\tab\tab\tab\tab assuming the position of Executive Vice President, she served as \par
\tab\tab\tab\tab Provost and Vice President for Academic Affairs at St. \par
\tab\tab\tab\tab Augustine\rquote s. In that role, Luckes was responsible for the \par
\tab\tab\tab\tab oversight and execution of education policy; supervision of \par
\tab\tab\tab\tab programs of instruction of faculty and instructional budgets, \par
\tab\tab\tab\tab and faculty and staff effectiveness and development. <br>\par
\tab\tab\tab\tab <br>\par
\tab\tab\tab\tab Luckes also held several higher education administration \par
\tab\tab\tab\tab positions at Hampton University, serving as Director of Human \par
\tab\tab\tab\tab Resources and Executive Assistant to the Executive Vice \par
\tab\tab\tab\tab President and Provost. She serves and takes great pride in her \par
\tab\tab\tab\tab volunteer work as a peer evaluator with the Commission on \par
\tab\tab\tab\tab Colleges, Southern Association of Colleges and Schools. Dr. Luckes is a member of Delta Sigma Theta \par
\tab\tab\tab\tab Sorority, Inc.<br>\par
