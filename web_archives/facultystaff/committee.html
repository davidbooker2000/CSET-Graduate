 University Space Committee
 
  The University Space Committee is an advisory body
to the President and Provost with the following primary responsibilities: 

»  Ensure an orderly and centralized management of
    classrooms

»  Coordinate the utilization of space requirements as
     they affect laboratory and co-curricular activities

»  Create and maintain a management utilization system
    for reporting all aspects of space

»  Evaluate requests for all teaching, research and
    faculty and administrative requirements

»  Recommend space assignments consistent with
    the institution’s overall priorities and requirements

»  Report annually to all external bodies on space
    utilization matters
