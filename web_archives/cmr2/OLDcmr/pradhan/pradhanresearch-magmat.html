Ferromagnetic properties of epitaxial manganite films on SrTiO3 /Si heterostructures

We report on the magnetic properties of epitaxial La0.7Ba0.3MnO3 and La0.7Sr0.3MnO3 films on Si (100) and Si (111) substrates using SrTiO3 template layer, which demonstrate magnetic and electrical properties at and above room temperature. The magnetization data show magnetic transition and magnetic hysteresis at and above room room temperature. The films show well-defined magnetic domains. The ferromagnetic resonance studies show anisotropic effects related to ferromagnetic properties of films. The smaller grain size of about 20 nm in manganite films on SrTiO3 /Si may be one of the reasons to minimize the strain effect through strain relaxation at the interface between SrTiO3 and manganites through the formation of three-dimensional islands.
