Undergraduate Research and Scholarship Program

CMR Undergraduate Research

Students receive monthly stipend, while participating in exciting research, and present results at national and international conferences.

Benefits

  • Work in cutting-edge research in advanced materials
  • One-on-one mentoring
  • $3,250 yearly stipend


Eligibility Requirements

  • 2.8 GPA
  • Physics, engineering or chemistry major
  • Submit completed application by deadline


Program Requirements

  • Maintain 2.8 GPA
  • Secure research mentor among CMR researchers
  • Work at least 10 h/week on research-related activities
  • Attend required meetings and conferences


CMR NASA-CREAM Scholarship
NASA provides ten prestigious scholarships to Norfolk State University chemistry, physics, and engineering majors.


Benefits

  • $3,500 per year
  • Research Internships


Eligibility Requirements

  • 3.0 GPA
  • Chemistry, physics or engineering major intending to pursue advanced degrees in the physical sciences
  • Submit completed Application by deadline


Program Requirements

  • Maintain 3.0 GPA
  • Attend required meetings and conferences
  • Be peer mentor to new CREAM Scholars
  • Maintain communication with program director

Specific requirements must be met to satisfy undergraduate scholarship eligibility:
  • The student must be admitted to Norfolk State University or a participating institution.
  • The student must enter as a freshman or as a student who has completed the Associate in Science degree.
  • The student must major in chemistry, physics, or materials science.
  • The student must have demonstrated potential in the sciences as evidenced by strength of high school coursework, GPA, SAT or ACT scores, and/or science club participation.

Application Dates

For early award notification, applicants should apply for admission to Norfolk State University or a participating institution and submit a scholarship application by the 15th of March.

Beginning in March, applications will be reviewed on the 15th of each month. Scholarships will continue to be awarded until funds are depleted.
