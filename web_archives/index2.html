College of Science, Engineering, and Technology (CSET) Masters and PhD Programs


The College of Science, Engineering and Technology is a dynamic School.  It remains, a major force for change within the University as an innovator and initiator of the most high demand and highly technological programs on campus.   The college offers three excellent Masters programs in the fields of Computer Science,  Engineering, and Materials Science & Engineering. These programs complement the Materials Science and Engineering PhD program.

The strategic mission of The College of Science, Engineering and Technology is to transform NSU to a "Science and Technology Powerhouse." Three centers of excellence have been established to support the science and technology mission. These centers are engaged in cutting edge research and provide many opportunities for student internships. Visit our Center for Materials Research (CMR), , Information Assurance Research Education Development Institute (IA-REDI),Center for Biotechnology and Biomedical Sciences

This site provides detailed information on science and technology programs and resources to help us attain "powerhouse" status. Please enjoy your visit.
